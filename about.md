## The research program

**The powers and limits of representation — studied with exact ground truth and honest ceilings.**

The choice of representation determines what is computable, learnable, and discoverable. I run
falsifiable, exact-ground-truth experiments to map where a representation *converts search into direct
access* (its power) and where it provably *cannot* (its limit).

**Who.** Micheal Ezeoda (`myketheguru` on GitHub, `_darkmyke` on socials) — a software engineer with
10+ years building real systems, with broad scientific curiosity, now doing independent foundational
research. The engineering background is the method: every claim is
a small, runnable, falsifiable experiment, and the code is the proof.

### Three tracks, two repos
- **Foundations & frontier** —
  [representation-dial](https://github.com/myketheguru/representation-dial): a progressive, runnable
  tour from *search is conserved* to *the access/search dial*. (Reproductions of established results —
  Kohlas, Schaefer, Kolmogorov, Pearl, No-Free-Lunch — credited, not claimed; plus the active frontier
  experiment.)
- **Concept-rediscovery** —
  [concept-rediscovery](https://github.com/myketheguru/concept-rediscovery): the original experiment —
  can a compression objective rediscover named human concepts from exact-label data? With honest
  nulls.

### The standard
Exact ground truth where possible; every claim a runnable demo; established results credited by name.
Engineering discipline pointed at foundational science.

### Reach out
Open to collaboration with people in interpretability, geometric/representation learning, and
complexity-aware ML who value ground-truth rigour over demos.
